# Benchly v2.12.2 — Frosted Glass contrast fix

A design-critique-driven fix for the Frosted Glass theme. Its panels were white-translucent,
which lightened the gradient behind them — so on bright backgrounds (and near the corner glow)
text and the lighter accents, especially the yellow "warn" pills, washed out and became hard to
read.

Panels are now a translucent dark "smoked glass": the customisable gradient reads as an ambient
glow around and through them, while text and the vivid accent colours sit on a dark backing and
stay legible on any background. The corner glow and backdrop saturation were toned down too.
